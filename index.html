IMPORTANCE OF BLOOD BANK

Blood banks are integral to modern healthcare, serving as repositories for blood and blood products that save countless lives. They collect, test, process, and store donations from volunteer donors, ensuring hospitals have immediate access to safe blood.

BLOOD DONATION PROCESS

The process starts with registration and health checkups. Blood is then drawn safely in a controlled environment. Donors are given refreshments post-donation.

TESTING & SAFETY PROTOCOLS

All collected blood is tested for HIV, Hepatitis, and other diseases. Strict safety measures are followed throughout the process.
